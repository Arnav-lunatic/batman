Origin:

Batman's alter ego is Bruce Wayne, a billionaire playboy and philanthropist. As a child, Bruce witnesses the murder of his parents, Thomas and Martha Wayne, in a mugging gone wrong. This traumatic event fuels his lifelong mission to fight crime and bring justice to Gotham City. Bruce travels the world, mastering various martial arts and detective skills, and eventually, he trains with the League of Shadows, led by Ra's al Ghul.

Becoming Batman:

Bruce Wayne returns to Gotham City and, inspired by a bat that flies through his window, decides to adopt the persona of Batman. He creates a bat-themed costume and uses his intellect, detective skills, and physical prowess to fight crime. Batman operates from the Batcave, a high-tech headquarters beneath Wayne Manor.
